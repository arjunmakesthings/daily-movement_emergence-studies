### 260611:

there is a <mark>world</mark>. the world is a container, and consists of many <mark>places</mark>.

the world contains <mark>beings</mark> — they are born, they live, and they die; over <mark>time</mark>. life is expressed in <mark>movement</mark>, and movement is governed by a <mark>schedule</mark>. 

---

however, what is presented to a person is not merely the system, but a <mark>software-interpretation</mark> of the system. this is what makes it different from a 'simulation'. 

choosing what to show for a particular software-interpretation is the result of artistic decisions taken by the maker — highlighting what they thought was worthy of attention.
