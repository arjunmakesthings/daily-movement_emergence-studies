### 260611:

there is a ==world==. the world is a container, and consists of many ==places==.

the world contains ==beings== — they are born, they live, and they die; over ==time==. life is expressed in ==movement==, and movement is governed by a ==schedule==. 

---

however, what is presented to a person is not merely the system, but a ==software-interpretation== of the system. this is what makes it different from a 'simulation'. 

choosing what to show for a particular software-interpretation is the result of artistic decisions taken by the maker — highlighting what they thought was worthy of attention.
